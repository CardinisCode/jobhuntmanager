Purpose

The Job Hunt Manager is a web-application where you can store all matters related to job hunting:

  • Job Applications
  • Upcoming Interviews
  • Interview Preparation
  • Take Notes
  • Contacts
  • Companies from your wish list

This specific version is a Pilot, and therefore is only intended as a proof of concept.

Background

As completion to the current Computer Science course, I was asked to design something that I believed would add value to a community of my choice. It could be a web application / phone application / or game, as long as I designed & developed it to completion. Although it was possible to co-develop this application with a fellow student, I found connecting with fellow students, during Covid19, to be rather challenging. So I designed & developed this entire application myself.

Over the past two years of self-teaching (with the mentoring from my partner), I've developed a passion for developing in Python. Although it may have been easier to use Django, I decided to rather use (and stick to) Flask. It gave me a lot of flexibility & challenges alike. I used WTForms for all the Forms - it was delightful and fairly easy to use.

I think of all the challenges I've come across, when developing this application, I think Scope-creep has been the hardest. I have yet to master it! Despite having a good idea of what I wanted to achieve, I quickly realised that the scope was far beyond the scope needed for this project. In fact, I could easily continue developing the application years into the future.

So I've decided to designate this application to be a pilot / proof concept, with the hope I can develop this application into a fully operational application in time.

Research

Before I even started working on the development for this application, I decided to actually find out if fellow job hunters would use such an application.

  1. Informally asked fellow job hunters on the Whatsapp job hunting groups if they would use the application.
  2. Put together a Google form and posted it both in the Whatsapp groups & on Linkedin.
  3. I asked a recruiter on Linkedin, who hires within the IT field.

9 people

I received strong support for the application from everyone I asked. What each person wanted from the application did vary, as can be expected.

Security

Each user's password is hashed and there are no administrators. So your details can only be seen by you and you alone. However no password salting has been used so please do not use your private information when utilizing this application.

Numerous fictional characters' details have been used during the testing process.
